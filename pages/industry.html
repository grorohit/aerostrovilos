Industries

The prospective clients for our product can be process industries like, paper textile, cement, etc for their combined heat and power. Sewage treatment plants can benefit from our generator to run their plant self sufficently. Sugar and Sabudana factories can use our technology to run their plant by using biogas from the wastes. Hotels can use our generators run by biogas from their kitchen waste and also provide air conditioning and or heating.
